The Best Movies According to Alex

My top 3 movies of all time.

Blade Runner: The Final Cut

The visuals and soundtrack really draw you into this seemingly simple but profound and thought-provoking story.

Alien

In space, no one can hear you scream. Indeed. Another Ridley Scott Masterpiece.

The Adventures of Ichabod and Mr. Toad

In particular, the Headless Horseman adaptation always spooked me as a kid and still does to this day.
